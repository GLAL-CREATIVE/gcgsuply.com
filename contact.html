Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard MOQ varies by product. For memory modules, typically 10-50 units; for SSDs/HDDs, 5-20 units; for GPUs, 1-5 units. However, we offer flexible terms for long-term partnerships and can discuss specific requirements.

How long is the warranty period?

All products come with full manufacturer warranty: Samsung/SK Hynix memory (3-5 years), Seagate/WD HDDs (3-5 years), Samsung SSDs (3-5 years), NVIDIA GPUs (3 years). We also provide technical support throughout the warranty period.

What are your payment terms?

For new customers: 30% deposit, 70% before shipment. For established partners: Net 30 terms available. We accept wire transfer (T/T), and can discuss other payment methods for large orders.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping time depends on destination and method: Express (DHL/FedEx) 3-7 days worldwide; Air freight 7-14 days; Sea freight 20-40 days. We maintain ready inventory for many products, reducing lead times.

Are your products genuine?

Absolutely. We only source products through authorized distributor channels. All items come with original manufacturer warranty and authenticity guarantees. We can provide certificates of authenticity upon request.
