About Global Creative Group

Bridging the gap between cutting-edge technology and business needs. Hong Kong-based, globally connected.

Who We Are

GLOBAL CREATIVE GROUP CO., LIMITED (環球創想集團有限公司) is a Hong Kong-based enterprise IT hardware trading company specializing in memory, storage, and GPU solutions. Founded with a mission to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and business needs, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for data centers, AI companies, and enterprises worldwide.

Our strategic location in Hong Kong positions us at the heart of global trade, enabling us to serve clients across Asia-Pacific, North America, Europe, and beyond. We maintain strong relationships with authorized distributors of world-leading brands including Samsung, SK Hynix, Seagate, Western Digital, and NVIDIA.

What sets us apart is our commitment to authenticity, competitive pricing, and exceptional service. Every product we supply comes with full manufacturer warranty, and our technical team provides professional support throughout the procurement process and beyond.
